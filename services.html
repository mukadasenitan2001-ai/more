About Renes Hicy

Two decades of supplying Lagos’ finishing trade.

Renes Hicy International Ltd. has supplied tiles, doors, plumbing fixtures, and interior finishing materials to homeowners, contractors, and developers across Lagos State for over 20 years.

We combine wholesale-scale sourcing with a showroom experience, so every client — from a single homeowner to a large estate developer — gets the same standard of material and service.

What We Do

Six core services, one point of contact.

Wholesale & Retail Supply

Bulk and per-unit supply of tiles, plumbing fittings, doors, and finishing hardware for estates, contractors, and retail buyers.

Tiling Services

Skilled installation teams deliver precision floor and wall tiling with durable, level finishes for residential and commercial sites.

Interior Consultation

Our specialists help you select materials, finishes, and colour palettes that match your space, budget, and project timeline.

Door & Staircase Installation

Supply and fitting of imported security doors, panel doors, and staircase railing built for durability and a refined finish.

WC & Bathroom Fitting

Installation of water closets, wash-hand basins, shower cubicles, and jacuzzis with precision plumbing and watertight finishing.

Custom Project Supply

Tailored material sourcing and delivery schedules for hotels, schools, malls, and large-scale residential developments.
